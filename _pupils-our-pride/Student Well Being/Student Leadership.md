<h3><strong>Student Leadership</strong></h3>
<p>At Temasek Primary, we believe every student has the potential to influence
others positively, to lead with heart and serve with pride, and uphold
the school values in their daily actions. Leadership is not just about
holding a position; it’s about making a difference. We aim to nurture leaders
with character.</p>
<p>Each leadership role in our school is purposefully designed with clear
responsibilities. Student leaders are thoughtfully selected and provided
with targeted training such as basic leadership training, peer support
leaders training, and CCA leaders training to help them grow in confidence
and carry out their duties effectively. They are given real opportunities
to practise leadership skills - whether through leading school events,
serving their peers, or initiating meaningful projects.</p>
<p>We take a whole-school approach to developing our student leaders. Teachers
and staff work closely with students, offering support, guidance, and platforms
for leadership in both formal and informal settings. Along the way, we
make it a point to affirm and recognise our leaders for their contributions,
knowing that encouragement plays a vital role in their growth.</p>
<p>Through this nurturing environment, we aim to develop every Temasekian
into a servant leader — one who is rooted in values, confident in action,
and ready to learn, lead, and serve both the school and the wider community.</p>

<p><strong><u>Events and Projects</u></strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Community Festive Outreach&nbsp;</strong>
</p>
<p>Each year, we will guide our student leaders in the planning and organization
of Community Outreach events to spread festive joy to the community. This
is also an opportunity for our students to hone their communication skills,
heighten their social awareness and sense of belonging to the community.
Throughout the year, our Prefects take the lead in engaging the student
population’s participation in this meaningful event. Teachers and students
reach out to the community during Chinese New Year, Hari Raya Puasa, National
Day and Deepavali.</p>
<p>&nbsp;<strong>Temasek S.H.A.R.E.S.</strong>
<br>(<em>Spreading Happiness And Regifting Essentials Selflessly</em>)</p>
<p>To nurture our student leaders in living out the school values, our Head
Prefect from 2024, Genevieve Wang, initiated the idea for our school to
care for the elderly community. Thus, &nbsp;we introduced the <strong>Temasek S.H.A.R.E.S.</strong> —
to encourage leading with compassion and purpose. This project provides
a meaningful platform for students to rally the school community to a common
cause.</p>
<p>In this initiative, our student leaders take the lead in encouraging staff
and students to donate useful items such as groceries, which are then distributed
to those in need. In 2025, under the leadership of our then Head Prefect,
Sophia Chan, the school came together to collect donated items for Lions’
Home for the Elders (Bedok). Our Prefects then delivered these items to
the elderly residents and interacted meaningfully with them by spending
time and putting up performances for them. This was truly a heartwarming
experience that reminds us all of the power of service, empathy and student
initiative.</p>
<p><strong>Leaders’ Investiture</strong>
</p>
<p>At our school, we believe in growing student leaders who lead with heart
and serve with pride. One keyway we invest in them is by guiding and supporting
them so they can contribute meaningfully to the school community.</p>
<p>Each year, we gather as a school to recognize and affirm the efforts of
our Student Leaders at the Leaders’ Investiture. It is a special occasion
that marks not only their appointment, but also the trust we place in them
to be role models for their peers.</p>
<p>Through this Leaders’ Investiture, we aim to:&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Recognise and affirm our Student Leaders for their service and contribution
to the school&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Honour our Primary 6 Student Leaders as they achieve a milestone in their
leadership journey&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Welcome our Primary 5 Student Leaders as they progress to be the senior
Student Leaders in the school, and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Congratulate all Student Leaders on their appointment</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>&nbsp;<strong>Student Leadership Training</strong>
</p>
<p>To help our student leaders understand their roles in influencing positive
change, we provide a range of leadership training programmes designed to
equip them with the mindset and skills needed to lead effectively.</p>
<p>These programmes include a mix of online modules and instructor-led workshops
tailored to their leadership roles. Through these experiences, our students
learn essential 21st-century competencies such as communication, teamwork,
decision-making and empathy.</p>
<p>&nbsp;<strong>Leadership Development Workshop</strong>
</p>
<p>Primary 3 to 6 Prefects, as well as Primary 5 and 6 CCA Leaders, attend
leadership workshops to strengthen leadership skills, boost their confidence,
and promote effective teamwork.</p>
